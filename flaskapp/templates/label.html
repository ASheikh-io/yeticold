Step 2: Label Your Equipment

Do following to label your equipment and then click next.

  • Using masking tap and a marker write the word "DISCONNECTED" on both the outside and the inside of one laptop and the words "DANGER - ONLINE" on the outside and inside of the other laptop.
  • Use a piece of tape to cover the camera and microphone of both laptops.
  • Using a marker write the word "LARGE" on your large external hard drive and "SMALL" on your smaller external hard drive.
