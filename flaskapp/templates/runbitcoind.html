Yeti Bitcoin Cold Storage

Step 7: Install Bitcoin and download the blockchain before clicking Next.

  • Yeti has opened bitcoin-qt for you. This is the graphical interface for bitcoin core and will provide more detailed status.
  • Once it has completed updating you will have a bitcoin core full node.
  • This step can take as little as 4 hours if you have an SSD and a fast internet connection or as long as a couple of weeks if you do not.
  • When bitcoin finished the "initial block download" it will show your balance in bitcoin-qt and the Next button will be enabled."
