Yeti Bitcoin Hot Storage

Step 11: Move Seed Words File to USB drives.

  1. Insert one of your 3 USB drives into the USB port of your laptop.
  2. Open the Files application and click on Documents.
  3. Copy the file name "yhseed.txt" to the external USB drive.
  4. Remove the USB drive and place it with one of your paper seed packets.
  5. Repeat steps 2 through 4 above with your other two USB drives and then click Next.
