Step 45: Copy Seeds to USB drives

  1. Open your "Documents" folder.
  2. Insert the USB drive labeled "1" and copy the file "seed1.txt" to it.
  3. Remove and then reinsert the USB drive.
  4. Open the file "ycseed1.txt" on the USB drive and make sure that it displays your seed words before removing it.
  5. Place the drive labled "1" in your "Seed Packet 1."
  6. Repeat steps 2 through 6 with the respective ycseed(x).txt file and usb drives.
  7. Return to your ONLINE laptop currently at step 44 and click Next to continue to step 46.
