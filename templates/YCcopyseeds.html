Step 26: Copy Seeds to USB drives

  1. Click on the Files application in the left side bar in Ubuntu and then click Documents to display your the electronic files that contain your seed words.
  2. Insert the USB drive labeled "1" and copy the file seed1.txt to it by dragging it from Documents.
  3. Remove and then reinsert the USB drive.
  4. Open the file ycseed1.txt on the USB drive and make sure that it displays your seed words.
  5. Place the drive labled "1" in your "Seed Packet 1."
  6. Repeat steps 2 through 6 with the respective ycseed(x).txt file and usb drives.
  7. Return to your ONLINE laptop currently at step 14 and click Next to continue to step 30.
