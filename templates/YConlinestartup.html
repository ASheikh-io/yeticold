Step 10: Copy Files to Disconnected Laptop

  1. Plug your Large external drive into your Disconnected laptop.
  2. Copy these folders into the Home directory on your Disconnected laptop.
    • yeticold folder.
    • .bitcoin folder.
    • ToDisconnected.tar.gz file.
  3. One the Disconnected laptop click on the 9 dots in the lower right corner, then click Terminal
  4. In the terminal window type the below text and click Enter<\>.
    5. python3 ~/yeticold/scripts/YetiColdDisconnected.py
  5. Yeti will then install all the required software and updates. It may take up to 2 hours to complete.
  6. When you see "Step 11: Further Randomize Seeds" on the Disconnected laptop leave this window open and continue with Step 11 on the Disconnected laptop.
