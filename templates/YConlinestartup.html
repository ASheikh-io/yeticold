Yeti Bitcoin Cold Storage

Step 10: Copy files to DISCONNECTED laptop

  1. Move your external hard drive to you DISCONNECTED laptop and copy these folders into the "Home" directory.
    • yeticold folder.
    • .bitcoin folder.
    • ToDisconnected.tar.gz file.
  2. One the DISCONNECTED laptop open a terminal, type the below and click Enter.
    3. python3 ~/yeticold/scripts/YetiColdDisconnected.py
  3. This will install all the required software and updates. It may take up to 2 hours to complete.
  4. When you see "Step 15: Start DISCONNECTED Yeti" on the DISCONNECTED laptop, leave this window open and continue with Step 15 on the DISCONNECTED laptop.
