Step 12: You Have Completed Yeti Hot Wallet Setup

You can now use bitcoin core to send and receive funds to your bitcoin full node.
  • If this computer is lost or damaged you can recover your bitcoin balance as long as you have one of your seed packets.
  • Bitcoin core does not support QR code scanning so a button to scan and display the contents of a QR code is provided below.
  • To send funds to a bitcoin address in a QR code simply scan the QR code and copy and paste the address into bitcoin core.
  • Be sure to select the wallet "yetihot" in the upper right hand corner of the bicoin core interface.
