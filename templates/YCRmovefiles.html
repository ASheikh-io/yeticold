Yeti Bitcoin Cold Storage

Step 13: Copy Files to the Disconnected Laptop

  1. If you copied these files to your Disconnected laptop after you last sent funds into this Yeti Cold storage location you can skip to Step 5.
  2. Connect your Large external hard drive to the Disconnected laptop.
  3. Open the Files application, click on the three horizontal bars button in the upper right corner and check the box to show hidden files.
  4. Navigate to your "Home" directory and copy these items to your external hard drive (click Skip All when prompted):
    • .bitcoin (a folder)
    • ToDisconnected.tar.gz (a zipped file)
    • yeticold (a folder)
  5. On the Disconnected laptop click the Nine Dots in the lower right corner of the desktop and then click Terminal.
  6. In the terminal window type the text below and click Enter.
    7. python3 ~/yeticold/rec.py
  7. When Step 14 displays on the Disconnected laptop leave this window open and continue with Step 14.
