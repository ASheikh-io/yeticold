Yeti Bitcoin Cold Storage

Step 8: Copy files to large external hard drive.

  1. Connect your Large external hard drive.
  2. Open the Files application, click on the three horizontal bars button in the upper right corner and check the box to show hidden files.
  3. Navigate to your "Home" directory and copy these items to your external hard drive (click Skip All when prompted):
    • .bitcoin (a folder)
    • ToDisconnected.tar.gz (a zipped file)
    • yeticold (a folder)
