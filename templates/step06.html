Yeti Bitcoin Cold Storage

Step 6: Start Yeti on your ONLINE laptop.

  1. Open the termimnal application by clicking the 9 dots in the lower left hand corner and then clicking on "Terminal."
  2. Inside the terminal application type "python3 ~/Downloads/onlineyeti.py" . You can paste into the terminal by right clicking and selecting Paste.
  3. When promted enter your password in the terminal.
  4. Type "y" and click Enter when prompted.
  5. When complete a browser window will open. If you get an "unable to connect error" in your browser wait a moment and click refresh.
  6. When you see the "Step 7: Install Bitcoin Core" page this tab and continue with step 7 in the other browser tab.
