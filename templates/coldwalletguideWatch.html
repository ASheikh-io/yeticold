Cold Wallet Guide

These steps will walk you through Reciving.

Receive: Step 1

In the bitcoin core application on your Online Laptop click the Receive button in the menu bar.

Receive: Step 2

Click the "Create new receiving address" button outlined in red.

Receive: Finished

Confirm that your wallet (outlined in red) shows "yetiwalletpriv"(offline laptop) or "yetiwalletpub"(online laptop). You can now send money to the wallet using the QR code or the address displayed bellow it.
