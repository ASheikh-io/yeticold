Step 17: Send Funds into Cold Storage

  • Send cold storage funds to one of these addresses and verify that the balance of watch only funds in Bitcoin Core increases.
  • Sending funds to multiple addresses improves your privacy as your total balance is more difficult to determine.
  • All of the 1,000 addresses show on the next page are made spendable with three of your seven seeds.
  • Before sending significant amounts you should verify that you can send and receive funds using any three of your seed words by repeating the recovery process.
