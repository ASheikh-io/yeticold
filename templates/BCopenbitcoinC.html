Step 9: Download the Blockchain

  • Yeti has opened Bitcoin Core for you and it will provide more detailed status.
  • Once it has completed downloading you will have a Bitcoin Core full node.
  • This step can take as little as 4 hours if you have an SSD and a fast internet connection or as long as a couple of weeks if you do not.
  • When bitcoin finishes it will reach 100% and then show your balance and the Next button will be enabled on this page.
  • Click Next when this is complete. You will see a new terminal window opened that asks for your password so that it can package your installed software to transfer to your Disconnected laptap.
