Yeti Bitcoin Cold Storage

Step 3: Copy Files to Large External Hard Drive.

  1. If you have an Yeti Cold Disconnected laptop setup already you can skip this step by clicking Next, unless you have sent funds into cold storage since the Disconnected laptop was setup.
  2. Connect your Large external hard drive.
  3. Open the Files application and check the box to display Show Hidden Files.
  4. In the next step you will see a warning that Ubuntu can't copy symbolic links. Click Skip All.
  5. Navigate to your "Home" directory and copy these folders to your Large hard drive:
    • .bitcoin
    • dpkg-repack
    • yeticold
