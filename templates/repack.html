Step 9: Package installed software

  • Yeti will now open a terminal window for you and ask for your laptop's password.
  • Then it will begin packaging all updates and sofware that have been added so that they can be transfered to your DISCONNECTED laptop.
  • Watch this terminal window as it may provide additional instructions.
  • This can take up to 3 hours to complete.
  • Once it is completed it will display "Done Repackaging."
  • Onec it is complete close the terminal window and then click Next.
