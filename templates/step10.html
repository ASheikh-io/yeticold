Yeti Bitcoin Cold Storage

Step 10: Copy files to DISCONNECTED laptap.

  1. Connect you LARGE external hard drive.
  2. Open the Files application and check the box to display hidden files and folders.
  3. Navigate to your "Home" directory and copy these folders to your external hard drive:
    • .bitcoin
    • dpkg-repack
    • yeticold
  4. Move your external hard drive to you DISCONNECTED laptop and copy these folders into the "Home" directory.
  5. One the DISCONNECTED laptop open a terminal, type "python3 ~/yeticold/offlineyeti.py" and click Enter.
  6. This will install all the required software and updates. It may take up to 2 hours to complete.
  7. When you see "Step 11: Start DISCONNECTED Yeti" on the DISCONNECTED laptap, leave this window open and continue with Step 11 on the DISCONNECTED laptap.
