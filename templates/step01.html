Yeti Bitcoin Cold Storage

Yeti Cold Storage

Yeti Cold Storage is for storing more than $50,000 USD worth of bitcoin. It is a 3 of 7 HD multisig setup where private keys are never on an internet connected device.

Yeti Warm Storage

Yeti Warm Storage is for storing between $5,000 and $50,000 USD worth of bitcoin. It is a 3 of 7 HD multisig setup that requries only a single computer to setup.
